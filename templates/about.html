OUR STORY

16+ Years of Excellence in Transportation

Gujrat Transportation began in 2007 with a simple vision: to make travel comfortable and accessible for everyone. What started as a small fleet of 5 buses has grown into a comprehensive transportation network serving millions of customers.

Today, we operate over 100 vehicles including luxury sleeper coaches, A/C buses, and heavy trucks, connecting 200+ cities across the nation. Our commitment to safety, punctuality, and customer satisfaction has made us the preferred choice for travelers and businesses alike.
