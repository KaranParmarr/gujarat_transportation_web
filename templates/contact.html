FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our Bus & Truck transportation services.

How do I book a bus ticket online?

Visit our website, choose route, date, seat type and complete secure payment.

What types of buses are available?

We offer A/C Sleeper, Non-A/C Sleeper and Tourist Seater buses.

Can I cancel my bus booking?

Yes, cancellations are allowed up to 6 hours before departure as per policy.

Are buses GPS tracked?

All our buses are equipped with real-time GPS tracking for safety.

Is night travel safe in sleeper buses?

Yes, trained drivers, safety checks and emergency systems ensure safe travel.

Do buses have charging points?

Most of our luxury buses include mobile charging points and reading lights.

Can I choose my bus seat?

Yes, seat selection is available during online booking.

Are food breaks included?

Yes, scheduled food and rest stops are included on long routes.

What types of trucks do you provide?

We provide Container, Dumper, Flatbed, Trailer and Logistics trucks.

Do you offer full-load and part-load trucks?

Yes, both FTL and PTL services are available nationwide.

Are your trucks insured?

All trucks are insured to ensure cargo safety during transit.

Can I track my truck shipment?

Yes, live GPS tracking is available for all truck consignments.

Do you transport fragile goods?

Yes, we provide special handling for fragile and sensitive cargo.

What documents are required for truck booking?

Basic ID, invoice, e-way bill and cargo details are required.

Is interstate truck transport available?

Yes, we operate across multiple states in India.

Do you offer contract-based logistics?

Yes, long-term and corporate logistics contracts are available.
